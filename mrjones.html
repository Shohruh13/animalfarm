Mr Jones's Perspective

About Power

Initially at first I thought I had so much power and control over these animals. I showed little to no care about that farm as I was commonly drunk and would drink a heavy amout of beer with me. I didn't feed the animals much and was actually struggling to keep the farm running because I was just too drunk.

Reacting to the Rebellion

If there was one thing that I didn't notice, was how smart some of these animals here actually are, and not in a good way. I realized that these animals were trying to revoke me and get rid of me to try and have a new leader. Soon enough, they actually succeeded. Soon later, I heard that the new leader was Napoleon, and he still treated them horribly, so what was the point anyways.
