About Mr Jones

Initially back before Napoleon took over, Mr jones was our own leader. He was the first person for us to realize that mankind was causing harm to all of us as we wern't getting fed enough or if we weren't treated well at all. This is when I had the idea to try and come up a way to rebel and make him no longer the main leader of the farm.

Reacting to Napoleon

As we were commiting the rebellion, I didn't actually expect someone to follow the actions of mankind even though I specifically told everyone not to do that. Sure that might have been my fault seeing as I only though everyone was going to act one way, although he should have known what he went through back when Mr. Jones was our leader and not replicate his actions.
